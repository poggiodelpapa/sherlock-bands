if you are not registered, please
register
(note: if at the time of registration you have not yet obtained your high school diploma, you must tick the "Waiting for High School Diploma" box)
log in
(SPID or CIE - Electronic ID card for Italian students; PSW and StudentIDnumber for international students);

How to upload your Isee
The Isee can be calculated at a Caf (tax assistance centre) or on the Inps website and is essential for determining the amount of your tuition fee.
The Isee calculation must be carried out at least 30 days before the pre-registration deadline (see next point).
The value to be requested is Isee 2025 for il
diritto allo studio universitario
(right to university education)
Procedure
Once your Isee has been calculated, you have to upload it to Infostud in the following way:
log in
(SPID or CIE - Electronic ID card for Italian students; PSW and StudentIDnumber for international students)
Go to Profile
Go to Isee
Go to Acquire Isee
The Isee can also be acquired on Infostud during the pre-registration phase.
Pre-enrolment - procedure for those who have successfully passed the first selection
If you have passed the first selection, you will have to pay a maximum amount of 200 euros (depending on your Isee income), which will be deducted from the first instalment.
Procedure
log in
SPID or CIE - Electronic ID card for Italian students; PSW and StudentIDnumber for international students)
go to Bachelor's and Master's degree programmes;
go to "Special procedures" and click on "Pre-enrolment Tolc-I";
Enter your data;
Click on "Enrolment". If your Isee is lower than the expected threshold, payment is not requested and you will be automatically included among the pre-enrolled students;
if your Isee is higher than the threshold, click on "Print", the payment slip will be created and you can proceed with payment;
click on PagoPA
(guidelines)
Once the payment has been made, there is no need to go to the Student Affairs Office or hand in the receipt.
